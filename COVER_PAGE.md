## Abstract

CAM/3iAtlas introduces a conceptual framework for interpreting large-scale cosmic morphology through observational reconstruction and image-informed modeling techniques. The framework explores structured approaches to analyzing deep-field astronomical imagery and proposes methodological foundations intended for exploratory analysis and future refinement.
